Samsung Galaxy S9

The Galaxy S9 is the only smartphone camera on the market that can turn you into an avatar. Samsung uses a data-based machine learning algorithm that analyses a 2D image of the user and maps out more than 100 facial features to create a 3D model of yourself. The benefits that came out of this phone are:
  • Brilliant display
  • Stereo sound is surprisingly powerful
  • Curved glass and metal are very attractive
  • Comfortable to use
  • Super speedy and fluid performance
