What is the Samsung Phone?

The samsung cell phone is one of the largest electronics companies in the world, which belongs to the South Korean Samsung Group. The Samsung phone is known for it's wide variety of digiyal media devices and many other systems.


Samsung Galaxy S

The Samsung Galaxy S which was released in 2010 was Samsung's first solid smartphone compared to the Bluetooth phone they had in cars. The Samsung Galaxy S was marked as a milestone when it hit over 1 million sales\shipments back in 2010. The launch price for the Samsung Galaxy S was $600 dollars, This phone also had a single core 1GHz processor and o.5GB of RAM. At the time, the galaxy S was the fastest graphical processing of any smartphone. It was also the thinnest smartphone at 9.9 mm (about 0.39 in), and the first Android phone to be certified for DivX HD. Although the Galaxy had good graphics Also the design of the phone was poorly.

Samsung Galaxy S6


The Galaxy S6 release price was about $600 dollars. The Samsung Galaxy S6 was one of Samsung’s bestselling phones in 2015, this phone like every other phone had its good and bad traits. The good thing about this phone is:

  • Great metal and glass design

  • Plenty of power for fluid and fast experiences

  • Excellent camera

  • Some grreat user inerface refinement

  • Great display

The galaxy S6 design had come up from the previous phone’s, the phone frame has been improved into a metal edge that borders the Gorilla Glass front and back. They also added the fingerprint scanner function for this phone instead of the “boring home button.” This phone ran on a Samsung-made octa-core processor, had 3GB of RAM and a 16-megapixel rear camera capable of recording Ultra HD footage. Samsung reached a milestone with this phone in 2015, getting positive reviews for the phone.

Samsung Galaxy S7 edge

The Galaxy S7 edge was named the “flagship phone” because the features of the phone were so much better than the others before it. Some features of this phone were:
  • Wonderful design and build quality
  • The edge curves look great
  • Good battery performance (better than S7 standard)
  • Camera performance
The milestone of this phone was how it offered a strong design and a quality of build that was top tier and that the quality stood strong in 2017. The Samsung Galaxy S7 and S7 Edge claimed 5.8 percent of U.S. smartphone sales in the first quarter despite not being released until March 11, and the galaxy S7 was one of the most promoted phones during 2015.

Samsung Galaxy S8

Samsung S8 was labeled another milestone for Samsung because of its improved design and added features. Stated by pocket-lint that “the Samsung Galaxy S8 is a masterpiece; one with a few brushstrokes out of place, but a masterpiece nonetheless.” Some of the good things about this phone were:
  • The most eye-catching phone design you can buy today
  • Great-looking 18.5:1 aspect ratio AMOLED screen is ideal for entertainment (even more so when HDR comes into play)
  • Best Samsung software experience to date
  • Decent battery life and optimizations
  • The rear camera is excellent
The S8 was also one of the first phones to introduce Infinity Display, that referred to the fact it had an edge-to-edge display with virtually no bezels. The design of the S8 was liked by many and most people really liked the lackluster Bixby features at launch, which allowed you to talk to your phone like Siri. They even improved the placement of the fingerprint sensor on the phone.

Samsung Galaxy S9

The Galaxy S9 is the only smartphone camera on the market that can turn you into an avatar. Samsung uses a data-based machine learning algorithm that analyses a 2D image of the user and maps out more than 100 facial features to create a 3D model of yourself. The benefits that came out of this phone are:
  • Brilliant display
  • Stereo sound is surprisingly powerful
  • Curved glass and metal are very attractive
  • Comfortable to use
  • Super speedy and fluid performance
