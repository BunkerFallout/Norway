Samsung Galaxy S6


The Galaxy S6 release price was about $600 dollars. The Samsung Galaxy S6 was one of Samsung’s bestselling phones in 2015, this phone like every other phone had its good and bad traits. The good thing about this phone is:

  • Great metal and glass design

  • Plenty of power for fluid and fast experiences

  • Excellent camera

  • Some grreat user inerface refinement

  • Great display

The galaxy S6 design had come up from the previous phone’s, the phone frame has been improved into a metal edge that borders the Gorilla Glass front and back. They also added the fingerprint scanner function for this phone instead of the “boring home button.” This phone ran on a Samsung-made octa-core processor, had 3GB of RAM and a 16-megapixel rear camera capable of recording Ultra HD footage. Samsung reached a milestone with this phone in 2015, getting positive reviews for the phone.
