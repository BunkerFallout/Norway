Samsung Galaxy S7 edge

The Galaxy S7 edge was named the “flagship phone” because the features of the phone were so much better than the others before it. Some features of this phone were:
  • Wonderful design and build quality
  • The edge curves look great
  • Good battery performance (better than S7 standard)
  • Camera performance
The milestone of this phone was how it offered a strong design and a quality of build that was top tier and that the quality stood strong in 2017. The Samsung Galaxy S7 and S7 Edge claimed 5.8 percent of U.S. smartphone sales in the first quarter despite not being released until March 11, and the galaxy S7 was one of the most promoted phones during 2015.
